## About Us

Tiwater is pioneering the next generation of AI agent platforms, with two flagship products serving distinct yet complementary domains:

- **Ticos** - Embodied AI platform for humanoid robotics and physical agents
- **Supen** - Enterprise AI platform for knowledge work and digital agents

## Products

<img src="https://cloud.ticos.ai/logo.svg" alt="Ticos Logo" width="60" height="auto">

### Ticos - Embodied AI Platform

**A comprehensive platform for developing and deploying intelligent humanoid robots.**

Ticos provides end-to-end solutions for embodied intelligence, from perception to action. Manage multimodal LLMs, voice recognition, vision systems, motion control, and terminal devices through a unified platform.

#### Key Capabilities

- **Perception** - Voice recognition with speaker identification, visual perception including gaze tracking
- **Intelligence** - Integration with commercial LLMs, custom models, and local deployments
- **Expression** - Voice synthesis, motion management with visual orchestration
- **Terminal Management** - Support for digital avatars, custom terminals, and humanoid robots

### Supen - Enterprise AI Platform

**AI-powered platform with integrated Vibex v3.0 multi-agent system.**

Supen revolutionizes enterprise workflows with natural conversation. Simply talk to X - your intelligent project representative that manages specialized agents behind the scenes. Perfect for research, writing, and knowledge management.

#### Key Features

- **Just Talk to X** - Single conversational interface to multiple specialized agents
- **Smart Research** - Context-aware information gathering and deep topic exploration
- **Document Magic** - Transform ideas into polished documents across multiple formats
- **MCP Integration** - Direct tool access via Model Context Protocol
- **Voice Preservation** - AI enhancement that maintains your unique style
